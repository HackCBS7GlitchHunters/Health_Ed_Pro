Test Levels:

  • 1. Test Questions (Mandatory):
    These are essential questions that all users must answer to complete the test. They ensure that the core information is gathered for an accurate assessment.
  • 2. Optional Questions (Skippable):
    These questions are not mandatory and can be skipped by users if they prefer. They provide additional insights but are not required for the main assessment.
  • 3. Final Result
    After completing the test, users receive their results based on their responses. The results provide valuable insights and recommendations tailored to their health needs.

Details of Source:

  • After users complete the mandatory and optional questions, they are provided with detailed information about the sources of the test questions.
  • This includes references to scientific studies, expert opinions, and credible health resources used to create the test.
  • By sharing these details, HealthEdPro ensures transparency, allowing users to understand the basis of their assessment and gain confidence in the reliability of the information provided.

Test Results:

  • Severity
  • Symptoms
  • Recommendations
