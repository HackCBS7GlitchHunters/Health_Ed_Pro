Please answer the following questions regarding Erectile Dysfunction (ED)

Please answer the following questions regarding your sexual functioning. Please answer the questions as honestly and candidly as you can. If any questions or terms are unclear, please ask for clarification.


(IIEF 1) Over the past four weeks, how often were you able to get an erection during sexual activity?

(IIEF 2) Over the past four weeks, when you had erections with sexual stimulation, how often were your erections hard enough for penetration?

(IIEF 3) Over the past four weeks, when you attempted sexual intercourse, how often were you able to penetrate (enter) your partner?

(IIEF 4) Over the past four weeks, during sexual intercourse how often were you able to maintain your erection after you had penetrated (entered) your partner?

(IIEF 5) Over the past four weeks, during sexual intercourse, how difficult was it to maintain your erection to completion of intercourse?

(IIEF 6) Over the past four weeks, how many times have you attempted sexual intercourse?

(IIEF 7) Over the past four weeks, when you attempted sexual intercourse, how often was it satisfactory for you?

(IIEF 8) Over the past four weeks, how much have you enjoyed sexual intercourse?

(IIEF 9) Over the past four weeks, when you had sexual stimulation or intercourse, how often did you ejaculate?

(IIEF 10) Over the past four weeks, when you had sexual stimulation or intercourse, how often did you have the feeling of orgasm or climax?

(IIEF 11) Over the past four weeks, how often have you felt sexual desire?

(IIEF 12) Over the past four weeks, how would you rate your level of sexual desire?

(IIEF 13) Over the past four weeks, how satisfied have you been with your overall sex life?

(IIEF 14) Over the past four weeks, how satisfied have you been with your sexual relationship with your partner?

(IIEF 15) Over the past four weeks, how do you rate your confidence that you could get and keep an erection?

Please answer the following questions regarding Premature Ejaculation Diagnostic Tool (PEDT)

1. How difficult is it for you to delay ejaculation?

2. Do you ejaculate before you want to?

3. Do you ejaculate with very little stimulation?

4. Do you feel frustrated because of ejaculating before you want to?

5. How concerned are you that your time to ejaculation leaves your partner unfulfilled?

6. Do you feel your sexual confidence is affected by your time to ejaculation?

7. Do you feel you have control over the timing of your ejaculation?

8. Does your time to ejaculation leave you feeling disappointment with your sexual life?

9. Does your time to ejaculation leave you feeling dissatisfied with your sexual life?
